Glow of Hope

by Sawlaram Haldankar

Glow of Hope, also titled "Woman With the Lamp", is a painting by S.L. Haldankar.The work was painted 1945-46 and is currently stored in the Sri Jayachamarajendra Art Gallery at the Jaganmohan Palace in Mysore, India.

Date: 1945-1946
Medium: Water colours
Movement: Modern Indian
Home: Sri Jayachamarajendra Art Gallery, Mysore
